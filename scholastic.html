Scholastic Analytics Challenge

january 2020

Background

This project was a team submission to the Scholastic Analytics Challenge hosted by the Association of Information Systems. My team and I were on a tight deadline as we only had two weeks to work on this project.

My team received a data set of two million records via a csv file. We cleaned, analyzed, and designed an infographic to report our findings. We were limited to submitting two pdf files, the infographic and a write up explaining our findings.

Python | Pandas Library | Tableau

Conclusion

When I started this project, I expected to be able to complete all the analysis on Excel and Tableau. Due to the size of the data set, I had no choice but to utilize Python. A lot of late nights were spent at the library in order to complete this project, but I do not regret my participation. This project ignited my passion for data analytics and allowed me to learn so much about perseverance along with the technical skills I gained.

Although my team did not place in the top 10, I am determined to tackle another analytics challenge in the near future with the new skills that I have gained through this project.
