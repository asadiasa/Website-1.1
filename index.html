Still Here!

Published: Aug 6, 2020

The day to day seemed to have slowly crawled on, but my summer break as a whole seemed to have flown by! I had a few things occupying my time but it was mostly a period of rest. Conciously carving out the time for rest and self-discovery is something that I have gotten to know these past 5 months. As a 20 year old, I am still trying to figure out who I am and my personal brand. This website has been through a handful of iterations as I explore web design and different tools. Most recently, I invested in an iPad and started using Procreate and PicsArt to play around with editing photos. As I continue my formal eduction, I hope to continue expressing my creativity through graphic design. No matter what you do, I am a strong believer that we all need an outlet for creativity!
